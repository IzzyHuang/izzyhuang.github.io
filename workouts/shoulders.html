Dumbbell Shoulder Press

>> Front Deltoid

3 Sets | 30 lbs | 10 Reps

Stand or sit with a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height, palms facing forward, and press the weights vertically overhead until your arms are fully extended, then lower back to the starting position.

Dumbbell Front Raise

>> Front Deltoid

3 Sets | 10 lbs | 10 Reps

Start seated or standing with dumbbells held in front of your shoulders, palms facing you; as you press the dumbbells overhead, rotate your hands so your palms face forward at the top, then reverse the motion when lowering back to start.

Dumbbell Lateral Raise

>> Lateral Deltoid

3 Sets | 10 lbs | 10 Reps

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, dumbbells at your sides, palms facing in; lift the dumbbells out to your sides with a slight bend in the elbows, raising them to shoulder height, then slowly lower back down.

Cable Rear Delt Fly

>> Rear Deltoid

3 Sets | 17.5 lbs | 10 Reps

Position the pulleys on a dual cable machine to shoulder height, grasp the handles with opposite hands (right hand to left pulley and vice versa), step into a staggered stance, and pull the handles outwards and back, keeping arms straight, then return to the start.

Bent Over Reverse Cable Fly

>> Rear Deltoid

3 Sets | 17.5 lbs | 10 Reps

Stand centered between the cables of a dual cable machine set to the lowest position, bend at the hips, grab the handles with opposite hands, and with a slight bend in the elbows, pull the handles out and up to shoulder height, then slowly return.

>> Rear Deltoid

3 Sets | 35 lbs | 8 Reps

Kneel on one knee in front of a high pulley, hold the cable handle with the opposite hand, pull the handle towards your lower chest while keeping your back straight and core engaged, then slowly extend your arm back.

Face Pull

>> Rear Deltoid

3 Sets | 35 lbs | 12 Reps

Attach a rope to a high pulley cable machine, grasp the rope with both hands, pull the rope towards your face while separating your hands, elbows high, then slowly return the rope to the starting position.
